Help

Welcome to Soothe, an AI-powered therapist simulation. Here’s how to use it:

  • Type your message and press Enter or click Send to chat.
  • Hover over your messages and click the edit icon below to edit them.
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+C or click the trash icon to clear the conversation.
  • Toggle between light and dark themes using the theme button.

Note: This is a simulation, not professional therapy.
